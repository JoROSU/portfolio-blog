PREVIOUS WORK

It starts and finishes with my passion for new ideas and understanding people

Customer segmentation & customer profiling

Integrating transactional and survey data to obtain and describe segments of customers though their representative members

I worked with FMCG transactional data and e-commerce data in combinations with psychological surveys. This kind of insights describe the customer base and point to the best ways of communicating with the target market. The better we know who the customers are, the better chances we have at creating a meaningful relationship with profitable customers.

Customer behaviour & behavioural change

Translating psychological concepts in big data can shed light on understanding customer behaviours such as impulsivity, habitualness and brand switching

I initially worked on integrating psychological theory and machine learning to develop a concept of Customer Change Modelling. The results of this model was a rank of the most relevant psychological and behavioural traits that are the most predictive of brand switching and initial purchasing of a brand.

Retention analysis & Brand storytelling

Every business should aim to detect and understand who their most profitable customers are. Doing retention analysis can also inform a company of the value of moving x-time customers down the customer funnel

More importantly, understanding which 'phase' of retention each customer is, helps us create personalisation with the goal of aligning brand storytelling with the world view of customes. This is the only way we can ensure we offer a unique solution to a particular problem a customer has.
